I am Hadi Sinaee, a graduate student at the University of British Columbia, Vancouver, Canada. My thesis was developing 2GP: Two-Phase Graph Partitioner which is a large graph partitioner. Previously, I received my M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence from the Sharif University of Technology, Tehran, Iran, where my research focused on Machine Learning.

As the dedicated CTO for approximately three years, those days were undeniably the most fulfilling and rewarding moments of my career, despite the eventual closure of the company. Moving forward, I transitioned to Ostadkar, the largest online platform for home services in Iran, where I assumed the roles of CTO and Director of Product. During this transformative experience, I successfully scaled the engineering and product teams from 5 to 30 persons within a six-month timeframe.
